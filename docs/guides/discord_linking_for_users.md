Linking your Discord account to ozfortress.com can be done in just a few clicks:

1. You have two options for reaching your [ozfortress.com](https://ozfortress.com) account settings. You can click the ":simple-discord: Start Linking Your Discord" button either above or below this short guide, or navigate to [ozfortress.com](https://ozfortress.com) in your browser, click on your username top-right, and clicking on Settings.
![Discord Linking #1](/img/discord_linking_1.png)
2. Under the Name Change field, you will see a Connections area with a Link Discord button. Clicking this will take you to Discord's Auth handler.
![Discord Linking #2](/img/discord_linking_2.png)
3. Here you should see "ozfortress wants to access your Discord Account" and you're Discord user. 
      - **You may need to sign into Discord on your browser if you aren't already signed in**, but once you have signed in, you should see this. You can view any permissions our services require listed on this screen.
![Discord Linking #3](/img/discord_linking_3.png)
4. Once you click Authorise, your account will be linked to our website! You should see a "Discord account linked" notification at the top of your User settings page. There is one more step however.
![Discord Linking #4](/img/discord_linking_4.png)
5. In any channel in our Team Captains Discord, you will need to run the `/sync` command. This links your Discord to ozfortress.com!

!!! warning "Please Note"
    **You're Discord ID will show on your user profile.** We are currently looking into hiding this to users who aren't logged in to ozfortress.com.

    ![Discord Linking #5](/img/discord_linking_5.png)
